SHIPPING & RETURNS

Delivery and return information

Shipping Information

Harpa Gaming ships worldwide from our distribution center in Miami, FL. Detailed shipping rates, estimated delivery times, and carrier information will be available once our online store launches.

info Our shop is currently under construction. Full shipping details will be published before our first product drop.

Planned Shipping Zones

  • United States (Domestic): Standard and express shipping options available.
  • European Union: Tracked international shipping with customs pre-clearance where possible.
  • Rest of World: International shipping available to most countries.

Returns Policy

We are committed to customer satisfaction. Our returns policy will include:

  • 30-day return window from the date of delivery.
  • Items must be unused, in original packaging, and in resellable condition.
  • Refunds processed within 5–10 business days after we receive the returned item.
  • Defective or damaged products eligible for free replacement or full refund.

Pre-Orders

Pre-order items ship on the estimated release date. If there are delays, we will notify you via email. Pre-orders may be cancelled for a full refund at any time before the item ships.

Questions?

For shipping or returns inquiries, contact us at cronereal@gmail.com or visit our Contact page.
