Shipping & Delivery

Yes. Harpa Gaming ships worldwide from our distribution center in Miami, FL. International shipping rates and delivery estimates will be calculated at checkout based on your location. For more details, see our Shipping & Returns page.

Domestic (US) orders typically arrive within 3–7 business days with standard shipping, or 1–3 business days with express shipping. International orders may take 7–21 business days depending on destination and customs processing.

Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your package in real time. If you have any issues with tracking, contact us at cronereal@gmail.com.

Returns & Refunds

We offer a 30-day return window from the date of delivery. Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and in resellable condition. For full details, visit our Shipping & Returns page.

To request a refund, contact our support team at cronereal@gmail.com with your order number and reason for return. Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item.
